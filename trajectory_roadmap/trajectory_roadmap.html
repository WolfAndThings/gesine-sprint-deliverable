The exit window · 2031-2033

Touchland's $880M is the lane.
Aesop's $2.525B is the ceiling.

Year 5 puts GESINE in the same archetype-and-revenue zip code as Hero Cosmetics and Touchland in their pre-exit years. Year 6+ optionality stretches into Drunk Elephant or Aesop territory. The trajectory only works because each year's moves stack — retail proof unlocks licensing leverage unlocks hospitality channel unlocks corporate gifting recurrence unlocks paid-scale CAC discipline unlocks exit-grade margin.

Hero ($630M, 2022) and Touchland ($880M, 2025) both went to C&D at ~5.5x sales. Same archetype: female-founded, design-led, scent-adjacent, EBITDA-positive, retail-proven. GESINE = the third in the series.
