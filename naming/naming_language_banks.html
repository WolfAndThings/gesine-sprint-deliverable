Language guardrails

What to say.
And what protects the brand.

EWG, FTC Green Guides, and EPA Safer Choice all leave room for confident language — as long as it's substantiated. The list below stays inside that lane and avoids the legal hot zones around health claims and unverifiable eco language.

Use freely
  • EWG-conscious formula
  • Plastic-free refill system (if substantiated)
  • Biodegradable formula (if substantiated)
  • Made without [specific ingredient]
  • Designed with safer home care in mind
  • Thoughtfully formulated
  • Refill-based system designed to reduce single-use plastic
  • Ingredient transparency
  • Mood, ritual, atmosphere, reset, calm, focus, transition, fresh start
Avoid without legal review
  • Chemical-free
  • 100% non-toxic / completely safe / harmless
  • No toxins / zero impact
  • Cures, treats, lowers, reduces (anxiety / depression / insomnia)
  • Therapeutic / medical-grade / clinically proven mood enhancer
  • Eco-friendly (without specifics)
  • Biodegradable (without conditions)
  • Cleans your air
  • Anything that implies a medical or psychological outcome
