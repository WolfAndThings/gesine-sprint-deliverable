01 Must-have today · in the meeting
Required
  • Trademark search status — has anyone filed for "GESINE"? USPTO TESS check done? Need date of filing or commitment to file this week.Reason: @ritueliahome competitor is closing on the same naming territory.
  • Brand name lock confirmation — is "GESINE" 100% locked? Or are alternatives still in play? Affects every doc + every domain decision.
  • Founder bios + photos — name, role, background, current title, headshots if comfortable. Drives the founder-story content angle and press positioning.
  • Decision-maker map — who approves creative? Who approves spend? Who signs Phase 2? One name per question.
  • Funding status — pre-revenue? Self-funded? Investor-backed? Round closed? Shapes how we sequence Phase 2 vs build-in-public.
  • Launch target date — Fall 2026 in the original brief — still accurate? Specific month or week?
  • $750 Phase 1 payment confirmation — Stripe link sent / paid? Or sending today? Sprint kicks off the day payment clears.
02 Product + formulation specs
Within 7 days
  • Bottle dimensions, materials, finish — exact measurements, glass type (smoked-amber? frosted?), cap material/color, sprayer mechanism. Locks visual prompts + investor deck mockups.
  • Refill vial dimensions + closure type — drives Refill Ritual still-life photography (deliverable D06 prompt 15).
  • Holder material + dimensions — ceramic? wood? metal? matte/gloss? Holder is critical — it earns counter space.
  • Bamboo towel specs — size, weave, color options, manufacturer or sourcing partner.
  • Packaging dimensions — outer box (book-style? rigid? magnetic?), insert tray material, total weight, ship-ready dimensions.
  • Full ingredient list per scent SKU, with INCI / CAS numbers if available. Required for the L'AVANT-style ingredient transparency page.
  • Fragrance ingredient disclosure — IFRA-compliant? Phthalate-free disclosure? Allergen list?
  • Scent formulations status — which scents are formulated vs concept-only? Lead notes per scent.
  • Surface compatibility test data — what surfaces is it tested on? Marble? Stone? Wood? Painted walls? Drives "thoughtfully formulated for the surfaces you touch every day" copy.
  • Efficacy testing results — anti-microbial, cleaning effectiveness vs Lysol-tier, third-party lab if any.
03 Claims + compliance
Within 7 days
  • EWG Verified status — applied? in process? not yet? "EWG-conscious" is safe; "EWG Verified" requires the certification.
  • SDS sheets (Safety Data Sheets) — one per scent SKU. Required for any retail or B2B placement.
  • Biodegradability substantiation — under what conditions? OECD test data? Time to biodegrade? Determines whether "biodegradable" is a usable claim or a legal landmine.
  • "Plastic-free" substantiation — refill vials material, cap material, label adhesive. One plastic component breaks the whole claim.
  • "Made without [list]" — confirm what's NOT in the formula. (Phthalates, parabens, ammonia, chlorine, bleach, dyes, etc.)
  • Approved legal claims list — anything reviewed by legal counsel that we can use verbatim?
  • Country-of-origin — where formulated? where bottled? where assembled? "Made in USA" / "Made in France" claims have different legal thresholds.
  • Certifications pursued or held — Leaping Bunny? Cruelty-free? B Corp? 1% for the Planet? Climate Neutral?
