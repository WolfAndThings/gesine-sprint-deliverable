Corporate gifting is the fastest path from launch to scale. Hotels, agencies, real estate, HR — every one of them is buying gifts they already know are getting trashed. GESINE solves the only metric that matters: does the gift stay on the desk? This board defines ten concepts, the buyer behind each, and the inquiry email that closes them.
◆ The opening market

Why gifting is the launch lane.

The US corporate gifting market grew from $258B in 2022 to a projected $312B by 2025 (Coresight Research). The pain is universal: buyers report 80% lift in retention and word-of-mouth when the gift is something the recipient actually keeps (Knack, 2024). GESINE is built to be the kept gift.

◆ Sales playbook

Three channels. One outbound motion.

Don't try to fight gifting concierges from day one. Win the buyer first, the platform second, the press third.

CHANNEL 01
Direct outbound
LinkedIn + warm-intro emails to People leads, brokerage owners, hotel ops, agency partners. Sample-first, deck-second.
3 personalized opens / day
Free sample shipped before the deck
Track to first reorder, not first order
CHANNEL 02
