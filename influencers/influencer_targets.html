How this list was built
Pulled live via Apify on April 29, 2026 using two actors: apify~instagram-scraper (to grab the most recent 30 posts from @flamingoestate, @touchland, @aesop, @blueland, @carawayhome) and apify~instagram-profile-scraper (to verify each tagged or featured creator). Hashtag-based searches were attempted but discarded — Instagram's hashtag pages now surface mostly small accounts and noise. Brand-tagged user data is the higher-signal path. The same Apify pipeline becomes Phase 3 of the AI Roadmap as a recurring "creator discovery" workflow inside Weavy + n8n: scrape brands weekly, append new tagged users to Airtable, auto-enrich, queue outreach.
