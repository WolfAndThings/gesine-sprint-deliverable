How GESINE scales creative, sales, ecommerce, and CX through AI
A brand with its own
AI nervous system.
Most $50M-funded brands run AI in scattered silos: an agency runs creative, a freelancer runs ads, ChatGPT writes captions, customer support copy-pastes. GESINE will run on a single visual node graph in Weavy.ai, with every brand asset, gifting pitch, content drop, and customer touchpoint passing through a private brand-intelligence layer that the internal team can edit without writing code.
The infrastructure stack

Built on Weavy.ai —
owned, internal, visual.

Weavy.ai is the base layer. It's a visual node graph (React Flow + WASM canvas) that lets the GESINE team chain prompts, image models, video models, scrapers, and APIs into reusable workflows — without engineering. Around it, three supporting tools handle delivery, storefront, and content operations.

Weavy.ai
Core infrastructure
Visual node graph for every brand workflow. Brand voice, claims, scent library, and approved language live here as reusable nodes. Image and video gen pipelines (NB2, ChatGPT, Veo 3.1, Seedance) plug into the same graph. Internal team edits flows directly — no code.
Lovable.dev (private)
Internal tools layer
Private, password-gated apps for the team only. Mockup generators, gifting deck builders, scent finder, refill recommender — built and rebuilt in hours, not weeks. Powered by Weavy outputs.
Shopify
Storefront + commerce
Public-facing ecommerce. AI-driven personalization fed from Weavy: scent finder, ritual builder, gift kit configurator. Klaviyo handles email, Postscript handles SMS. Both feed back into Weavy for content reuse.
Airtable + n8n
Content + ops glue
Airtable holds the content calendar, gifting prospect list, scent SKU registry. n8n schedules posts, fires gifting outreach, syncs SKU updates. Weavy generates assets, n8n distributes them.
The six phases

Each phase is a
Weavy graph, not a project.

Every phase below is a Weavy.ai workflow the GESINE team can run on demand. Once built, they don't go stale — they get refined, branched, and combined. Phases compound.

Phase 01
Brand Intelligence Base
Foundation · Weavy core graph
A central brand RAG node holding voice, approved claims, banned phrases, scent library, ingredient story, audience profiles, competitor references, and visual rules. Every downstream workflow pulls from here. Updates propagate everywhere automatically.
Outputs
  • Brand voice + tone tokens
  • Approved claim library
  • Scent + ritual taxonomy
  • Reference photo + palette set
  • Banned-language guardrails
Phase 02
Visual Mockup Engine
Creative · NB2 + ChatGPT + Veo 3.1
A graph that takes a scene brief and outputs a finished GESINE-branded image or short video. Used for: investor deck visuals, retail shelf mockups, gifting concepts, collab packaging, social assets, landing-page heros. Internal team types one line and gets ten variations.
Outputs
  • Product-in-room scenes
  • Branded gifting flat lays
  • Retail shelf mockups
  • Collab packaging concepts
  • 5–10 sec social videos
Phase 03
Corporate Gifting Sales Engine
B2B revenue · Apollo + Weavy + Lovable
Input one company name. Output: custom mockup, scent recommendation, mini pitch deck, outreach email, branded landing page. Apollo enriches HR / brand-partnerships contacts. Lovable spins a one-off page per pitch. The sales team ships ten branded proposals before lunch.
Outputs
  • Per-company mockup + scent rec
  • Custom pitch deck
  • Cold-outreach email + follow-up
  • Branded mini landing page
  • Enriched contact list
Phase 04
Content Engine
Social · One campaign → 8 channels
A single campaign idea (e.g. "The 9pm Nightstand Reset") becomes Instagram captions, TikTok scripts, Pinterest pins, LinkedIn B2B posts, email subject lines, blog draft, Shopify section copy, founder voice-over. n8n schedules the drops. Klaviyo fires the email.
Outputs
  • IG / TikTok / Pinterest assets
  • LinkedIn B2B post variants
  • Email subject + body
  • Shopify section copy
  • Blog + SEO draft
Phase 05
Ecommerce Personalization
Conversion · Shopify + Lovable + Weavy
Customer-facing AI tools embedded in the Shopify storefront. Scent finder quiz, ritual builder, gift kit configurator, refill reminder, custom corporate kit builder. Each is a Lovable.dev micro-app fed by Weavy outputs. AOV and conversion lift come from giving the buyer a system, not a product.
Outputs
  • Scent finder quiz
  • Ritual builder
  • Gift kit configurator
  • Custom corporate kit builder
  • Smart refill reminders
Phase 06
Customer Experience AI
Retention · Brand-aware support layer
A brand-aware AI agent (running on the same Weavy brand-intelligence base) that handles FAQs, scent education, surface-compatibility questions, refill-subscription edits, gifting concierge, post-purchase ritual follow-ups. Same voice as the website. Same claim guardrails as the marketing copy.
Outputs
  • FAQ + onboarding bot
  • Scent education flow
  • Refill subscription concierge
  • Corporate gifting intake
  • Post-purchase ritual cadence
How phases compound

Every phase makes the
next one cheaper.

The sequencing matters. Each phase outputs nodes, prompts, or training data that the next phase reuses. By Phase 5, the Visual Mockup Engine isn't being asked to start from scratch — it's drawing on a brand brain, a scent library, and a thousand prior on-brand outputs.

Compounding chain Phase 01 brand brain → feeds every prompt in 02–06
Phase 02 mockup library → trains style consistency for 03 + 04
Phase 03 gifting outputs → become Phase 04 content campaigns
Phase 04 content prompts → graduate into Phase 06 customer-facing voice
Phase 05 personalization data → retrains Phase 01 brand brain
Phase 06 CX transcripts → surface unmet needs & Phase 02 mockup gaps
