The marketing ecosystem under the AI roadmap
Nine tools, one nervous system.
Founder sleeps. Brand still ships.
The AI Roadmap defines what GESINE will build. This page defines how it actually runs day-to-day. Nine tools wired into a single loop: Weavy creates, n8n orchestrates, Airtable remembers, Blotato distributes. Every campaign writes itself into the database. Every database row teaches the next campaign.
The nine layers

Built on tools you can
own, swap, or self-host.

Each layer has one job. None of them try to do everything. The architecture stays maintainable because every piece can be replaced without breaking the rest.

Layer 01 · Creative
Weavy.ai
Make the asset
Visual node graph. Brand RAG, image gen (NB2/ChatGPT), video gen (Veo/Seedance), copy gen, scent library. Internal team edits flows without code.
Layer 02 · Orchestration
n8n
Move the asset
Self-hosted workflow engine. Webhooks, schedules, custom code nodes. Fires Weavy graphs, writes to Airtable, posts via Blotato, sends Klaviyo emails. The system's spine.
Layer 03 · Memory
Airtable
Remember everything
Single source of truth. Content calendar, gifting CRM, influencer pipeline, product feedback, refill subscriptions, scent performance. Every workflow writes a row.
Layer 04 · Distribution
Blotato
Post the asset
Multi-platform poster. IG · TikTok · Pinterest · LinkedIn · X · Facebook · YouTube · Threads. Scheduled drops, native uploads, status verification. One API, every channel.
Layer 05 · Commerce
Shopify
Sell the asset
Storefront + checkout. Webhooks fire on order, abandoned cart, refill due. Personalization tools (scent finder, ritual builder) embedded as Lovable apps.
Layer 06 · Email
Klaviyo
Send the email
Transactional + marketing email. Welcome series, refill reminders, gifting inquiries, founder voice newsletter. Copy drafted in Weavy, personalized via Klaviyo segments.
Layer 07 · Internal Tools
Lovable.dev
Spin a tool fast
Private mockup generators, gifting deck builder, scent finder quiz, custom corporate kit configurator. Hours to build, hours to iterate. Powered by Weavy outputs.
Layer 08 · Scraping
Apify
Pull external data
Instagram + TikTok hashtag/profile scrapers, competitor product pages, Sephora/Amazon reviews, boutique hotel directories. Feeds creator pipelines, gifting prospect lists, product feedback loops.
Layer 09 · B2B Enrichment
Apollo
Find the buyer
Enrich corporate gifting prospects. HR contacts, brand-partnerships leads, boutique hotel GMs. Powers the gifting sales engine.

The standard campaign flow

12 steps from brief to insight.

Every campaign GESINE runs follows the same loop. Once built once in n8n, it runs unchanged for every future drop. The team's job becomes briefing and approving — not posting.

One campaign · End-to-end
01
Founder logs brief into Airtable / Campaigns
02
n8n watches the table and fires the Weavy campaign graph
03
Weavy generates: caption variants, image set, short video, hashtag map, email subject lines
04
Outputs land in Airtable / Posts as draft
05
Founder reviews in Airtable kanban view, edits or approves
06
Approval flips status to scheduled, posting time chosen
07
n8n picks up scheduled rows at post time, uploads media to Blotato storage first
08
Blotato fires posts to chosen platforms (IG, TikTok, Pinterest, LinkedIn, etc.)
09
Blotato webhook returns success → Airtable status posted, post URLs logged per platform
10
Apify scrapes engagement metrics at 24h and 7d marks
11
Performance data writes back to Airtable / Posts, joined to original campaign brief
12
Weekly: Weavy reads top performers, drafts the next campaign brief, loop restarts
Airtable as the brain

Seven bases, one truth.

Every workflow above writes to one of these. The brand is queryable. Founder asks "show me every gifting touch this quarter that converted" — Airtable answers in two clicks.

Content Calendar
posts · campaigns · assets · captions
Every drop, every variant, every channel. Status: draft → scheduled → posted → tracked. Kanban view for founder review.
Gifting CRM
companies · contacts · pitch · revenue
Corporate prospects. Stage pipeline, mockup ID, scent rec, deck URL, reply, value. The B2B revenue dashboard.
Influencer CRM
creators · outreach · UGC · conversion
Every creator scraped, contacted, sent product, posted, converted. Engagement tracked, attribution measured.
Product Feedback
reviews · sentiment · cluster · action
Aggregated reviews from every retailer + DTC. AI-clustered into themes, prioritized by frequency × severity.
Press
outlets · writers · pitch · coverage
Beat-mapped writers. Pitches sent per beat, replies tracked, coverage clips logged with backlinks.
Refill / Subscription
customer · cycle · status · churn
Every subscription, every refill, every lapse. Predictive nudge timing, churn-risk scoring, win-back queue.
Scent Performance
SKU · sales · quiz match · reorder
Per-SKU dashboard. Sales × quiz match rate × review sentiment × reorder rate. Tells GESINE which scent to scale, retire, or remix.
Brand Brain (Weavy meta)
approved claims · banned phrases · voice tokens
The legal-safe and on-brand layer Weavy reads from. Updated once, propagates everywhere — every email, every caption, every CX reply.
