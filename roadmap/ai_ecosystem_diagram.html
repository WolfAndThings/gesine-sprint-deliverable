Three jobs.

The same nine-tool diagram serves three operational lanes. Each lane is a different graph in Weavy + a different cron in n8n + a different base in Airtable.

Lane 01 · Email
Klaviyo + Weavy
Welcome series, refill reminders, abandoned cart, gifting inquiry routing, founder voice newsletter. Weavy drafts copy from the Brand RAG. Klaviyo personalizes per segment. n8n triggers based on Shopify webhooks and refill cycles.
Lane 02 · Product feedback
Apify + Airtable
Sephora + Amazon + Shopify reviews scraped weekly. Weavy clusters into claim / complaint / idea / praise. Per-SKU sentiment scoring. Reformulation briefs back to fragrance house. The product gets smarter with every order.
Lane 03 · Outreach
Apollo + n8n + Blotato
Influencer + corporate gifting + press + hotel + real estate pipelines. Apollo enriches contacts. Weavy generates per-target pitch + mockup. n8n sends 3-touch cadence. Reply triggers calendar booking. Every touch logged in Airtable.
