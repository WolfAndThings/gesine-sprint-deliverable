◆ What we say, in what order, to whom
Three audiences. One brand voice.
Zero mention of cleaning.
GESINE earns its premium by refusing the language of cleaning. Investors hear category creation, consumers hear ritual, retailers hear gifting. The product remains the same; the headline changes. This framework is the operating manual for every line of copy from launch through retail rollout.

◆ Five ad hooks

The five angles we test on day one.

Five hooks, five 30-second ad arcs. Each maps to a buyer persona and a paid channel. $1K each, 14-day test, win criteria in the landing/ad plan.

AD 01 · Designed to be seen
"You don't hide an Aesop bottle. Why hide your cleaner?"
CTA: Shop the bottle
Sub-line
Cleaning, designed to live on the counter.
Persona target
Design-Wellness Millennial
30s ad arc
0–3s: Aesop hand wash on a marble counter, warm light. 3–8s: Pan to a sad plastic Clorox bottle, beat. 8–18s: Sweep transition — GESINE bottle replaces the Clorox. Hand sprays linen. 18–28s: Three SKU lineup. 28–30s: Logo + "Designed to be seen."
AD 02 · Cleaning as ritual
"What if cleaning felt like lighting a candle?"
CTA: Start the ritual
Sub-line
A scent-led home reset, not a chore.
Persona target
Sunday Reset Buyer
30s ad arc
0–4s: Hand striking a match, candle lights. 4–10s: POV woman walks through morning kitchen. 10–20s: She picks up GESINE bottle, sprays, wipes once, breathes. 20–28s: Counter reveal — bottle stays out next to the candle. 28–30s: Tagline: "A ritual, not a chore."
AD 03 · Safer home
"Plastic-free. EWG-conscious. Designed for a home you live in."
CTA: Read the ingredients
Sub-line
Every ingredient disclosed. Every bottle refillable.
Persona target
Clean Home Buyer
30s ad arc
0–5s: Toddler hand on a marble counter. 5–12s: Slow scroll over the ingredient list (full disclosure on screen). 12–22s: Refill ritual — the bottle staying, the refill cartridge replacing. 22–30s: "Designed for a home you live in. Not just a home you photograph."
AD 04 · Corporate gifting
"The branded gift they keep on their desk."
CTA: Inquire about MOQ
Sub-line
Co-branded scent. Co-branded bottle. One inquiry away.
Persona target
Premium Gift Buyer · B2B HR/agency
30s ad arc
0–5s: Stock pile of cheap branded mugs — cut. 5–12s: Single GESINE bottle on a designer desk, brand foil visible. 12–22s: Three real client logos co-branded (mock). 22–30s: "A gift that doesn't end up in a drawer."
AD 05 · Scent-led dopamine
"The reason she actually cleans now."
CTA: Start with a scent
Sub-line
Eight scent rituals. One bottle. The chore is the byproduct.
Persona target
Sunday Reset Buyer · Design-Wellness Millennial
30s ad arc
0–3s: UGC creator: "I have a confession." 3–10s: "I never used to clean — until this." 10–22s: Spray demos across nightstand, desk, kitchen. 22–28s: Scent flight — eight bottles on the counter. 28–30s: "Pleasure first. Cleaning, second."
