# Color Palette: 3 Directions To Pick From

Lee + Ben pick one for the GESINE house palette. The C&B exclusive Smoked Sage colorway sits on top regardless of which house wins, per [research/crate_barrel_intel.md](research/crate_barrel_intel.md).

These are three distinct directions, not three flavors of the same thing. Pick one, commit, the brand world flows from it.

## Option A. Apothecary Warm

Lane: Aesop-adjacent. Old apothecary, new pricing. The bone + brown + accent oxblood that signals heritage.

| Role | Color | Hex `[unverified — design pass needed]` |
|------|-------|------|
| Primary surface | Bone | `~#EDE6DA` |
| Object body | Espresso | `~#3D2E22` |
| Accent | Oxblood | `~#6B1B25` |
| Type / hardware | Antique brass | `~#9B7E4C` |

Reads as: heritage, ritual, library. The bottle looks remembered, not designed.
Risks: too close to Aesop. Risks blending into the apothecary tier already crowded.
Best fit if: investors want "luxury heritage" framing first.

## Option B. Architectural Mono

Lane: Apple Pro Display + MoMA Design Store. Chalk, charcoal, pewter. No warmth, no nostalgia. Object is mathematical.

| Role | Color | Hex `[unverified]` |
|------|-------|------|
| Primary surface | Chalk | `~#F4F2EE` |
| Object body | Charcoal | `~#2A2A2C` |
| Accent | Pewter | `~#8A8A8E` |
| Type / hardware | Anodized titanium | `~#5C5C5E` |

Reads as: design-object first, cleaning second. Belongs in the same conversation as a Braun radio or an Apple Mac Studio.
Risks: cold, hard to warm up for editorial press shoots. Could read clinical if not styled with people in frame.
Best fit if: brand wants to live in the design-museum + tech-aesthetic conversation.

## Option C. Coastal Cool

Lane: 1 Hotels meets Edition meets the C&B Smoked Sage already locked. Bleached linen, fog, salt. Coastal-luxury without becoming "beach house."

| Role | Color | Hex `[unverified]` |
|------|-------|------|

Reads as: hospitality-grade, ritual-paced, calm without going monastic.
Risks: tonal danger zone. Without strong typography contrast, can disappear on retail shelf.

## Smoked Sage stays on top of any lane
