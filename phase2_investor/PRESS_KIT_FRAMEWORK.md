# Press Kit Framework

What journalists, retailers, and partners receive when GESINE goes live.

## What the kit contains (digital + physical)

### Digital folder (Google Drive or microsite)

1. **One-page press release** - 250 words max. Launch news, founder line, hook.
2. **Founder bio** - Lee + Ben. 100 words each. Headshots attached separately.
3. **Brand story long-form** - 600 words. Origin, market gap, design philosophy.
4. **Fact sheet** - SKU list, prices, materials, where to buy. One page.
5. **Image pack** - 12 hero images at print res (300dpi), 12 at web res. Captions and credits embedded.
6. **Video assets** - 3 cuts: 60s ritual, 30s product, 15s social. Captioned versions included.
7. **Quote sheet** - 5 ready-to-quote founder lines, organized by topic (origin, design, market, future).
8. **Press FAQ** - 8 standard questions with sanctioned answers.
9. **Logo pack** - SVG, PNG transparent, mono and color, all sizes.
10. **Brand color and font reference** - one-page

### Physical kit (top-tier press only)

- Bottle (Smoked Sage colorway)
- Vial set (3 scents)
- Wood box, linen ribbon
- Handwritten note from Lee
- Single-card brand origin

Sent to: 25 hand-picked editors and journalists at launch.

## Press release template

```
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
[date]

[BRAND NAME] LAUNCHES [TAGLINE]
[Subheading: one specific newsworthy line]

[CITY] - [Date] - [Brand], the [category description] founded by [founders], 
today launched [product], a [specific design or innovation lead].

[Quote from founder, 25 words. Voice the market gap, not the feature.]

[Key facts: launch date, retailers, price range, where available.]

[Quote from a partner or retail buyer if available.]

[About the brand: 50 words. Origin in one sentence. Mission in one sentence. 
Roadmap in one sentence.]

For samples or interviews, contact: press@gesinehome.com

###
```

## Founder quotes (5 ready-to-use)

To be drafted with Lee. Topics:

1. **Origin** - What gap they saw and why now
2. **Design** - Why the bottle looks the way it does
3. **Market** - Why luxury cleaning is white space
4. **Refill / sustainability** - Why object-led cleaning is more sustainable
5. **Future** - Where the brand goes in 3 years

## Press FAQ (8 standard)

1. Why launch a luxury cleaning brand now?
2. How is GESINE different from Method, Mrs. Meyer's, Homecourt?
3. Who is the customer?
4. What is the price range and why?
5. Where is the product manufactured?
6. What is the refill model?
7. Who are the founders?
8. What is the next product or category?

(Answers drafted with Lee + Ben.)

## What success looks like (PR side)

- 1 Tier 1 lifestyle feature within 90 days (AD, Vogue Living, Cup of Jo, Strategist)
- 3 Tier 2 editorial features within 90 days
- 5 trade press mentions (BeautyMatter, Modern Retail, Glossy, BoH, AD Pro)
- 1 podcast appearance (How I Built This, Acquired, BoF, second-tier startup show)
- 1 creator-driven viral moment (TikTok or IG, 1M+ views)
