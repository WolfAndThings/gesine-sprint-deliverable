# Risk Register

Honest accounting of what could go wrong. Mitigation per risk. Investors expect this. Lee should review with Ben before any pitch.

## Severity scale

- **High**: existential or material to thesis
- **Medium**: slows growth, eats margin, but survivable
- **Low**: nuisance, fixable in a sprint

### 5. Hazmat / regulatory failure on cleaning concentrate

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Severity | High |
| Probability | Low-Medium |
| Why | Cleaning products fall under EPA, state-level (CA Prop 65), DOT classifications for shipping. Concentrates can be ground-only or air-restricted. International shipping triggers more rules. `[unverified — needs Ben + counsel review of formula classifications]` |
| Impact | Forced reformulation, label rework, recall, fines, retail delisting. |
| Mitigation | (1) Engage regulatory counsel before formula lock. (2) Get EPA + state-level review of every active ingredient. (3) Confirm DOT classification per concentrate type. (4) Run third-party safety audit. (5) Carry product liability insurance. |
| Owner | Ben + regulatory counsel |
