## 1. Calm Partnership History (2018-2026)

### American Airlines (Sept 2018)
- **Structure:** Content licensing / paid placement. AA was the FIRST airline partner for Calm. Three sessions custom-built for AA seatback and personal device viewing. Calm soothing nature scenes integrated into AA boarding videos.
- **Initiator:** AA approached Calm post-Apple "App of the Year" 2017 win. Headspace was already on Virgin Atlantic + 11 carriers, so AA needed a differentiated partner.
- **Term / size:** Not disclosed publicly.
- Source: [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2018/09/25/american-airlines-offers-meditation-app-calm-to-stressed-out-passengers.html), [APEX](https://apex.aero/articles/american-airlines-partners-calm-flight-relaxation-content/)

### Lincoln Motor Co. (Aug 2020 - present, 5+ years)
- **Structure:** Co-branded SKU + content. Started as "Lincoln Retreat" content series; expanded into "Rejuvenate" in-vehicle theme; in June 2025 launched "Elevated Journey" — a Lincoln-exclusive in-vehicle Calm app on Lincoln Digital Experience.
- **Sessions branded jointly:** "Recharge on the Road," "Gratitude on the Go," etc. Lincoln owners get complimentary 1-yr Calm Premium.
- **Term:** 5+ years, multi-renewal. Initiator: Lincoln (chasing Mercedes/Audi wellness positioning).
- **Deal size:** Not disclosed.
- Sources: [Lincoln Media Center 2020](https://media.ford.com/content/lincolnmedia/lna/us/en/news/2020/08/12/the-lincoln-retreat-lincoln-calm-collaborate.html), [Lincoln Media 2025](https://media.lincoln.com/content/lincolnmedia/lna/us/en/news/2025/06/26/lincoln-and-calm-bring-mindfulness-to-the-road.html), [MediaPost](https://www.mediapost.com/publications/article/406981/lincoln-partners-with-calm-for-in-vehicle-meditati.html)

### American Express (2020 - present)
- **Structure:** Cardmember benefit (1-yr free Calm Premium for new users; discounted renewal). Plus physical Centurion Lounge wellness areas (Houston) co-branded.
- **Initiator:** Amex (wellness-perk arms race vs. Chase Sapphire).
- Sources: [Amex News 2022](https://about.americanexpress.com/all-news/news-details/2022/American-Express-x-Calm-Share-Wellness-Tips-for-a-Mindful-Travel-Journey/default.aspx), [TravelDailyNews](https://www.traveldailynews.com/regional-news/american-express-and-calm-open-new-health-wellness-space/)

### Xbox / Microsoft (Dec 2022)
- **Structure:** Content swap + bundled subscription. Calm built first-ever video-game-soundscape meditations using Halo Infinite + Sea of Thieves audio. Game Pass Ultimate subs got 3 months free Calm Premium + 50% off year 1.
- **Initiator:** Xbox (mental-health positioning for gamers).
- **Note:** No co-branded controller. Offer expired May 2024.
- Sources: [GeekWire](https://www.geekwire.com/2022/video-game-fans-be-calm-xbox-partners-with-relaxation-app-to-offer-immersive-soundscapes/), [Pure Xbox](https://www.purexbox.com/news/2022/12/xbox-partners-with-calm-free-trial-now-available-as-game-pass-perk)

### Hilton (March 2025)
- **Structure:** Calm content integrated into Hilton's Connected Room TV/IoT system across portfolio. Closest analog to a CPG-style placement at scale.
- Source: [Hilton Newsroom](https://stories.hilton.com/releases/hilton-and-calm-partner-to-address-wellness-demand)

### Adidas, Paramount (ongoing)
- Cited by Calm's new Head of Licensing Joe Lawson as flagship deals; details not public.
- Source: [License Global](https://www.licenseglobal.com/lifestyle/10-minutes-with-calm-as-it-charts-a-licensing-driven-path-to-brand-expansion)

### Lululemon — NOT a partner of Calm-the-app
- The Lululemon "CALM" reference is to UK suicide-prevention charity Campaign Against Living Miserably. Different org.
- Source: [Lululemon GWB Partnerships PDF](https://corporate.lululemon.com/~/media/Files/L/Lululemon/our-impact/gwb-partnerships-list-en-2024.pdf)

## 3. Headspace Partnership History
- **Nike (2020-present):** Exclusive meditation/mindfulness/mental-training partner. Audio-guided runs hosted by Coach Bennett + Andy Puddicombe baked into NRC and NTC apps. Branded event activations. ([Nike News](https://news.nike.com/news/nike-headspace-partnership), [Headspace](https://www.headspace.com/partners/nike-partnerships))
- **Spotify (2016):** Spotify's first-ever joint subscription bundle; Headspace + Spotify Premium SKU. Distribution-driven. ([Music Ally](https://musically.com/2016/11/03/spotify-debuts-first-joint-subscription-with-headspace/))
- **Sesame Workshop (2020-2024):** Started small (audio collab), scaled to 45M YouTube views, Random House book line (6 SKUs ages 2-5), "Goodnight, World!" podcast, Emmy nomination. Best precedent for content-into-physical-product expansion. ([Sesame Workshop](https://sesameworkshop.org/about-us/press-room/headspace-and-sesame-workshop-team-help-kids-wind-down-goodnight-world/), [storyarb case study](https://www.storyarb.com/blog/scaling-small-collabs-into-big-wins))
- **Airlines:** Headspace was first to airlines (Virgin Atlantic 2011, scaled to 11 carriers including United + Delta). ([Skift](https://skift.com/2019/11/15/meditation-apps-battle-for-airline-partners/))

**Calm vs Headspace style:** Headspace = embedded content inside partner apps/products (Nike app, Spotify SKU). Calm = cardmember/benefit perks + lifestyle integrations (Amex, Lincoln, Hilton). Calm has just hired a licensing exec to push toward physical co-branded SKUs; Headspace has done physical (Random House books) first.

## 5. Co-branded Scent / CPG Collabs

### Heinz x Absolut (April 2023, Tomato Vodka Pasta Sauce)
- £2.50/350g jar, limited edition, sold via heinztohome.co.uk + Waitrose only.
- **Result:** 55 units/store/week vs 7-unit category avg. Range overtook ketchup as Heinz #1; +52% sales bump in 2 months; 24% Waitrose distribution.
- **Why it worked:** Both brands kept identity; co-typography on jar; #AbsolutelyHeinz OOH in London. Limited-edition framing drove urgency.
- Sources: [Marketing Week](https://www.marketingweek.com/heinz-absolut-turbocharge-sales/), [The Drum](https://www.thedrum.com/news/2023/05/31/inside-the-viral-heinz-x-absolut-tie-up-sent-pasta-sauces-sales-soaring), [Pernod Ricard](https://www.pernod-ricard.com/en/locations/uk/media/absolut-vodka-partners-heinz-launch-new-limited-edition-pasta-sauce)

## 7. Wellness-app x Physical-Product Precedent
- **Headspace x Random House** (Sesame books) — closest direct precedent: app-brand authority licensed into physical product, distributed via category leader.
- **Headspace x Sleep Number / Calm x XPel / Calm x bath products:** No public source for any of these.
- The closest physical-product Calm has done: Lincoln in-vehicle SKU + Amex Centurion Lounge wellness rooms. Both are channel placements, not retail CPG.
- **Bottom line:** A Calm-branded physical CPG SKU on retail shelves does not yet exist. Calm is actively seeking exactly this (per License Global / Joe Lawson hire). GESINE arrives at the right moment.

## 8. Cost of Entry
- **No public rate card.** Calm partnerships are bespoke. Industry comps for tier-1 wellness-app brand partnerships:
  - App-bundle/perk deals (Amex, Xbox): typically rev-share + co-marketing minimum, **no public source on dollar figures**.
  - Co-branded content series (Lincoln): usually low-six-figure to low-seven-figure annual content fee + co-marketing commitment, **estimated, no public source**.
  - Physical co-branded SKU (Headspace x Random House model): standard licensing royalty range 5-12% of wholesale + advance, **estimated based on licensing industry norms, no Calm-specific source**.

Founder narrative deck, 250 words, no slides. Internal product photography of refillable bottle + scent capsule on real bathroom counters.

**Months 3-4 (July-Aug):** Warm-intro hunt. Map Calm cap table against GESINE's. Identify 3 mutual-investor routes. Cold-warm Joe Lawson via LinkedIn with one specific ask: 20-min discovery call about whether a wellness physical-ritual SKU fits Calm's licensing roadmap. Parallel: get Crate & Barrel buyer to host a Calm intro lunch.

**Months 5-6 (Sept-Oct):** Discovery. Joint-creative session with Calm content team (modeled on Lincoln "Recharge on the Road" workshop). Define what a Calm-branded GESINE Organizer Set looks like: which scent capsule, which Calm session ID lives on the bottle (QR-linked Daily Reset, 5 min), what's on shelf vs Calm-app exclusive.

**Months 7-9 (Nov 2026-Jan 2027):** Term sheet. Standard licensing royalty (5-12% wholesale) + co-marketing minimum + content rights for one Calm session. GESINE owns SKU, manufacturing, distribution. Calm provides session, name, marks, and one in-app feature window.

**Months 10-12 (Feb-Apr 2027):** Build + launch. Q1 2027 production run sized to Crate & Barrel forecast + 1 hospitality channel. Soft launch via Calm's email list (target subset) and Crate & Barrel endcap. Full retail Q2 2027.

**Risk to flag for investors:** Calm has never done a retail-CPG co-branded SKU. GESINE may be the test case. Treat the deal as upside, not gating. Build the brand to stand alone.
