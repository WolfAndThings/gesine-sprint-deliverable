# Crate & Barrel Intel — GESINE Partnership Research

**Prepared:** May 4, 2026
**Context:** C&B locked as first retail partner for 2027 nationwide GESINE launch. Organizer Set ($78), exclusive Smoked Sage colorway, exclusive scent. Founder = Lee.

---

## 1. Parent Company & Corporate Structure

- Operating entity: **Euromarket Designs Inc.**, dba Crate & Barrel. HQ: Northbrook, IL. Founded 1962 by Gordon and Carole Segal. ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crate_%26_Barrel))
- **Otto Group (Hamburg, Germany)** acquired majority stake 1998, became sole owner 2011. Otto is one of the world's largest mail-order/e-commerce groups. ([Otto Group company page](https://www.ottogroup.com/en/ueber-uns/konzernfirmen.php?company=crate-and-barrel.php))
- Holding structure: **Crate and Barrel Holdings** owns four banners — Crate & Barrel, CB2, Crate & Kids, **Hudson Grace**. ([PR Newswire — Hudson Grace acquisition](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/crate-and-barrel-announces-acquisition-of-hudson-grace-300870024.html))
- Footprint: 89 stores + 8 outlets US/Canada, 15 international franchise locations. 7,500 employees across C&B + CB2. ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crate_%26_Barrel))
- Revenue: ~$1.25B (crateandbarrel.com 2025 sales, ECDB); group-wide reporting puts holdings at ~$1.7B. ([ECDB](https://ecdb.com/resources/sample-data/retailer/crateandbarrel))
- Digital share of business: **>60%**, per CEO Janet Hayes. ([Business of Home — CEO interview](https://businessofhome.com/articles/more-stores-a-restructure-and-trade-perks-crate-barrel-s-ceo-talks-big-plans))

## 4. Vendor Terms — Industry Benchmark (no specific C&B disclosure)

C&B publishes a **CBH Vendor Operations Guide** ([2026 PDF](https://www.crateandbarrel.com/assets/Vendor/VOG/CBH_VendorOperationsGuide.pdf)) covering compliance, labeling, EDI, chargebacks, but payment terms and slotting are negotiated bilaterally and not disclosed publicly.

Specialty home-goods benchmarks Lee should anchor on:

- **Payment terms:** Net 60 standard for specialty retail; Net 30 only for proven smaller brands; Net 90 demanded by larger department stores. Early-pay discounts (e.g., 2/35 Net 60) common. ([Cotton Inc retail terminology](https://www.cottonworks.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/11/1-2_Terminology_of_Retail_Pricing_1.pdf))
- **Markdown allowance:** 5–10% of wholesale typical for first-year specialty vendors. ([Smyth Retail markdowns](https://www.smythretail.com/general-retailing/markdowns/))
- **Gross margin target retailer-side:** 40–55% (specialty) — assume C&B keystones at ~55% so wholesale ≈ $35 on a $78 SRP. ([Eagle Rock CFO benchmarks](https://www.eaglerockcfo.com/blog/research/retail-chain-finance-benchmarks-2026))
- **Slotting fees:** Generally not standard at specialty home; replaced by co-op marketing contributions (5–8% of wholesale).
- **MOQ for nationwide rollout:** 89 stores × 12–24 units initial set ≈ **1,000–2,200 units** plus DC stock; **no public source — ask C&B contact** for exact.
- **Chargebacks:** routine for label/EDI/delivery-window violations per VOG.

- **Social** — IG-led with designer-collab posts; partner brand tagged for cross-amplification.
- **PR** — every named collab gets PR Newswire release + BoH/HFN coverage.

Co-op funding: **no public source — ask C&B contact**; specialty norm is 5–8% of wholesale into joint marketing fund.

## 6. AOV & Customer Demographics

- 2025 conversion rate 2.0–2.5%; AOV not publicly broken out but ECDB implies **~$200–$300 AOV** given volume + transaction counts. ([ECDB](https://ecdb.com/resources/sample-data/retailer/crateandbarrel))
- Age skew: only 6% in 18–24; majority 25–54. ([ECDB](https://ecdb.com/resources/sample-data/retailer/crateandbarrel))
- Income: 26% low-income shoppers (per ECDB), **majority middle-to-upper-middle HHI $75K–$150K** based on Placer.ai chain profile. ([Placer.ai](https://www.placer.ai/chains/crate-and-barrel))
- Core shopper persona: female, 30–50, registered or recently nesting; high cross-shop with Williams-Sonoma, West Elm, Pottery Barn.
- Specific HHI/cadence breakdown: **no public source — ask C&B contact** for proprietary segmentation.

## 7. Home Care Category — Current Assortment & Pricing

C&B's [scented candles & diffusers PLP](https://www.crateandbarrel.com/decorating-and-accessories/scented-candles-and-diffusers/1) carries:

- **44 scented candles**, **38 reed diffusers**, plus essential oils, room sprays, **dish soaps, countertop sprays**.
- Scent families: Lemon Zest + Tangerine, Lavender + Eucalyptus, Fig + Amber, Sea Salt + Citron.
- Pricing tiers:
  - Room sprays: $24.95–$49.95 reg
  - Reed diffusers (e.g. [Sea Salt + Citron](https://www.crateandbarrel.com/sea-salt-citron-diffuser/s302901)): mid range
  - Scented candles: $36–$84
  - Taper sets: $34.95–$109.95

GESINE Organizer Set at **$78** slots cleanly into existing price architecture. Smoked Sage colorway differentiates (current SKUs lean cream/amber/citrus). Cleaning-meets-fragrance crossover is a **white-space play** — C&B carries scented dish soap/countertop spray in coordinated kits, but no premium ritual brand owns the category.

## 8. Hudson Grace Overlap

- Acquired June 2019. ([Retail Dive](https://www.retaildive.com/news/crate-barrel-acquires-home-decor-brand/557276/))
- Founded 2012 by Monelle Totah + Gary McNatton (still leading). 10 stores, premium tabletop + entertaining, "coastal California" lean. ([Business of Home](https://businessofhome.com/articles/how-hudson-grace-went-from-local-shop-to-nationwide-boutique))
- Smaller, edited, higher-AOV — closer match to GESINE's ritual luxury positioning than mainline C&B.
- **Phase 2 expansion target: yes.** Hudson Grace shopper indexes higher on tabletop + entertaining + gifting; an 8 oz hand soap or candle co-pack would fit a HG endcap. Pitch HG buyer **~6 months after C&B launch** once sell-through data is in.

## 9. Loyalty Program — Crate & Barrel Visa / Rewards

Relaunched **October 2025** with **Imprint** as issuer ([BusinessWire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20251010215504/en/)):

- **10% back in Reward Dollars** at C&B, CB2, Crate & Kids, Hudson Grace
- 4% at other home + grocery + apparel (through 3/31/26)
- 1% everywhere else
- $50 welcome bonus after $1K spend outside the four banners in 90 days
- $100 milestone bonus at $10K annual spend
- Special-event access (double-rewards days), 6/12/24-mo financing
- No annual fee; one-for-one redemption

Practical implication for GESINE: cardholders are repeat, high-LTV C&B shoppers. Reorder behavior on consumables (refills) is the highest-leverage hook in the system. If GESINE's Organizer Set links to refills, cardholders earn 10% back on every refill — drives subscription-style behavior C&B currently lacks in this category.

## GESINE C&B Launch Playbook — 6 / 3 / 1 Month Out

**6 months out (Nov 2026)**
1. Lock VOG compliance: EDI, GS1 barcoding, label spec, packaging dims. Build a buffer DC inventory of **2,500 units** Organizer Set + Smoked Sage SKU + scent variant (cover 89 stores × 24 units + 30% safety stock).
2. Negotiate co-op marketing fund (target 6–8% of wholesale) earmarked for catalog spread, dedicated email send, in-store endcap fixtures at top-25 stores.
3. Pitch a named-founder narrative (Lee) — C&B's collab playbook is designer-led; lean into Lee as a craft/ritual creator, not a generic DTC brand.
4. Secure PR Newswire announcement slot tied to a holiday adjacency (Mother's Day or Spring Cleaning 2027).

**3 months out (Feb 2027)**
1. Lock NYC Flatiron in-store activation — pop-up ritual demo with Lee for 1 weekend; this is C&B's highest-traffic, highest-conversion door.
2. Train 30 in-house designers on Smoked Sage colorway story so they recommend Organizer Set in client consultations (Trade Program funnel).
3. Co-create email lifecycle assets: launch send, abandon-cart trigger, refill-reminder cycle. Offer Crate & Barrel Visa cardholders early access (48 hours) — drives loyalty data + 10%-back behavior.
4. Seed 50 designer/influencer kits via the C&B PR team, not GESINE's. Use C&B's pre-vetted IG list for credibility lift.

**1 month out (Apr 2027)**
1. Finalize endcap fixture install in top-25 doors. Smoked Sage palette must dominate — C&B's home-fragrance wall today is cream/amber.
2. Catalog ship: confirm spread placement + scan code to Organizer Set PDP.
3. Press embargo lift coordinated with C&B comms — target WWD, Business of Home, Vogue Living, House Beautiful.
4. Pre-brief Hudson Grace buyer for Phase 2: deliver 30/60/90-day sell-through data the moment C&B shares it; pitch HG endcap drop with a tabletop-adjacent SKU (small candle or hand soap) by Sept 2027.
