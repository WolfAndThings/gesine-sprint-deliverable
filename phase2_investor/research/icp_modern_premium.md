## Persona 1. Wellness-led Professional (Single / DINK, urban)

**HHI band.** $100K-$250K. Goop's core audience averages $100K+ HHI ([CanvasBusinessModel Goop profile](https://canvasbusinessmodel.com/blogs/target-market/goop-target-market)). 61% of DINK households earn $100K+ ([Fortune Nov 2024](https://fortune.com/2024/11/21/what-is-a-dink-dual-double-income-no-kids-new-1-percent-spending-luxury-travel-retail/)). 23.9% of all US households cleared $150K in 2023 (Census P60-282) ([Census 2024 release](https://www.census.gov/library/publications/2024/demo/p60-282.html)).

**Age band.** 25-44. Glossier's largest visitor cluster is 25-34 ([Similarweb glossier.com](https://www.similarweb.com/website/glossier.com/)). Aesop's core sits 25-44, with the brand's own profile skewing 20-55 educated urban ([Osum target audience teardown](https://blog.osum.com/aesop-target-audience/)). Goop's original target was 25-45 with $100K+ HHI ([CanvasBusinessModel](https://canvasbusinessmodel.com/blogs/target-market/goop-target-market)).

**Household composition.** Single urban professional or DINK. DINKs are 5% of US households per Harris Poll Nov 2024 and spend 4x more on dining ($816 vs $215/mo) and ~$2,000/vacation ([Fortune](https://fortune.com/2024/11/21/what-is-a-dink-dual-double-income-no-kids-new-1-percent-spending-luxury-travel-retail/)).

**Where they shop.** Aesop (395 stores in 29 markets at acquisition; sales went $28M to $537M 2012-2022) ([BeautyMatter](https://beautymatter.com/articles/will-aesop-become-a-billion-dollar-brand-for-loreal); [Modern Retail](https://www.modernretail.co/operations/following-its-acquisition-by-loreal-aesop-continues-its-retail-footprint-expansion/)). Goop.com, Sephora premium, Credo, Violet Grey. Direct DTC for Glossier.

**Monthly spend on home / wellness / aesthetic.** McKinsey: US wellness market is $480B, growing 5-10% YoY; Gen Z + millennial = 36% of population but 41% of wellness spend ([McKinsey 2024 wellness](https://www.mckinsey.com/industries/consumer-packaged-goods/our-insights/the-trends-defining-the-1-point-8-trillion-dollar-global-wellness-market-in-2024)). 82% of US consumers rank wellness top/important priority. Per-household monthly home + wellness discretionary line item: scrape pending — no free public source breaks this out for the $100K+ urban DINK band specifically.

**Brand affinities.** Aesop, Le Labo, Diptyque, Goop, Glossier, Necessaire, Public Goods. Goop newsletter readers averaged age 34, $100K+ HHI ([Wonder consumer profiling](https://start.askwonder.com/insights/need-demographics-psychographics-if-possible-goop-readers-followers-mci4b01jl)).

**Content sources.** IG + TikTok dominant. 90% of Gen Z and 84% of millennials say social content influenced a purchase in last 6 months ([Sprout Social 2025 generational report](https://sproutsocial.com/insights/press/new-research-from-sprout-social-finds-social-media-is-the-top-place-gen-z-turns-to-for-search-surpassing-traditional-search-engines/)). 41% of Gen Z search social before Google. Publications: The Cut, Vogue, Man Repeller successors, The Strategist, Cup of Jo. Podcasts: scrape pending — no public ranking by HHI.

**Purchase triggers.** EWG Verified mark functions as "gold standard of clean safety" per industry write-ups ([Clean Beauty Cos.](https://cleanbeautycompanies.com/ewg-verified-what-it-means-and-what-it-doesnt/)). 46% of US consumers (53% Gen Z) spent more on cosmetic procedures in 2024 than 2023 (McKinsey).

**Refill subscription willingness.** Auto-refill subscription models grew 26% YoY, 47% of consumers choose refillable packaging, willingness to pay 12-18% premium for sustainable options ([Mordor Intelligence household cleaners](https://www.mordorintelligence.com/industry-reports/household-cleaners-market)).

## Persona 2. Aesthetic-forward Homemaker (Millennial mom, design-driven)

**HHI band.** $150K-$200K. Anthropologie's profile cites 28-45 college-educated professional women, married, kids, $150-200K HHI ([Brand Hopper Anthropologie marketing teardown](https://thebrandhopper.com/2024/08/18/a-deep-dive-into-marketing-strategies-of-anthropologie/)).

**Age band.** 28-45. Anthropologie.com largest visitor cluster 25-34 ([Similarweb anthropologie.com](https://www.similarweb.com/website/anthropologie.com/)). Crate & Barrel skews older (Statista 2018 data shows 35-44 + 45-54 dominance) ([Statista C&B by age](https://www.statista.com/statistics/231386/people-who-shopped-at-crate-und-barrel-in-the-last-12-months-usa/), paywalled abstract).

**Household composition.** Married, 1-3 kids, suburban or urban-suburban. Anthropologie's stated profile: 30-45 woman, married with kids, professional or ex-professional ([Brand Hopper](https://thebrandhopper.com/2024/08/18/a-deep-dive-into-marketing-strategies-of-anthropologie/)).

**Where they shop.** Crate & Barrel (Furniture/Homeware = 95% of 2025 sales) ([ECDB C&B](https://ecdb.com/resources/sample-data/retailer/crateandbarrel)). Anthropologie home, West Elm, Pottery Barn, McGee & Co, Jenni Kayne Home, Boll & Branch, Schoolhouse, Brooklinen. Whole Foods + Erewhon for groceries. Williams-Sonoma + Food52.

**Monthly spend on home / wellness / aesthetic.** Millennials spend ~$1,771/yr on home decor (~$148/mo), 23% above Boomers ([SwiftBeacon home decor stats](https://swiftbeacon.com/blogs/news/home-decor-statistics)). Millennials spent average $12,101 on home projects in 2024 ([Angi State of Home Spending](https://finance.yahoo.com/news/millennials-driving-future-home-spending-140500004.html)). Average US household furniture spend: see [Statista US furniture 2024](https://www.statista.com/statistics/305566/us-expenditure-on-furniture/) (paywalled).

**Brand affinities.** Brooklinen, Boll & Branch, Parachute, Le Labo Santal 33 candles, Diptyque, Maison Louis Marie. For non-toxic clean: Branch Basics, Blueland, Grove (sub demographics scrape pending — no public investor disclosure breaks subscriber HHI).

**Content sources.** Pinterest dominant for home decor research. 47% of millennials buy furniture online after weeks of research; 75% check prices at multiple retailers; >50% of millennial moms use social to inform purchases, ~50% influenced by influencers ([iposos millennial 2024](https://iposos.com/millennial-shopping-habits-and-buying-trends-affecting-sales-in-2024/)). Publications: Domino, Architectural Digest, Apartment Therapy, Magnolia Journal. IG accounts: @studiomcgee, @amberinteriors, @athenacalderone. TikTok: #cleantok, #homereset, #sundayreset.

**Purchase triggers.** Sustainability + ethical production are significant decision factors for millennial home shoppers ([Capital One Shopping millennial stats](https://capitaloneshopping.com/research/millennial-shopping-statistics/)). EWG Verified resonates strongly with mom segment given child-safety framing ([EWG Verified](https://www.ewg.org/ewgverified/)). Crate & Barrel 2027 placement is the credibility marker — millennial moms research multi-retailer and trust C&B taste curation.

**Refill subscription willingness.** Strong. Same Mordor data applies (47% choose refillable, 12-18% premium tolerance). Mom segment over-indexes given household consumption volume + cabinet-aesthetic motivation. Branch Basics + Blueland subscriber economics confirm ([The Bossy Sauce comparison](https://www.thebossysauce.com/thebs/natural-cleaning-subscriptions-review-honest-grove-blueland-cleancult-thrive)).

## Persona 3. Hospitality / Amenity Buyer (Boutique hotel ops + Airbnb host)

**Segment size.** Global boutique hotel market $26.68B in 2024, projected $40.26B by 2030, 7.2% CAGR ([Grand View Research](https://www.grandviewresearch.com/industry-analysis/boutique-hotel-market-report)). US Airbnb hosts earned $24B+ supplemental in 2024; typical host earned ~$14,000; entire-unit avg $305/night ([Airbnb 2024 economic impact](https://news.airbnb.com/economic-impact-2024-us/)).

**Buyer profile.** Two sub-segments: (a) boutique hotel GM / procurement lead sourcing FF&E and amenities; (b) Airbnb / STR host operating 1-10 properties. Boutique buyers source via specialty procurement (R-W Purchasing, Beyer Brown, SLX Hospitality, Astria) for handcrafted + premium amenity SKUs ([R-W boutique procurement](https://rwpurchasing.com/boutique-lifestyle-hotel-procurement/); [SLX Hospitality](https://www.slx-hospitality.com/)).

**Where they buy.** Hotel toiletries from premium brand-name suppliers (APOTECAROMA, EAU D'EDEN, Beekman 1802, White Label) ([Amenities Depot wholesale](https://www.amenitiesdepot.net/blogs/news/wholesale-hotel-amenities-supplier)). STR hosts buy on Amazon, Costco, direct DTC for premium positioning. Minoan platform connects STR hosts to home brands for in-unit shoppable experiences ([Minoan amenities](https://minoan.com/blog/airbnb-amenities)).

**Monthly spend per property.** Average consumables ~$4.27/night per Airbnb host calc (2019, low confidence — likely understated for 2026) ([Airhosts forum](https://airhostsforum.com/t/how-much-do-consumables-cost-you/30493)). Boutique hotel: greatest 2024 cost increases were agency commissions (6.0%) and complimentary F&B (3.9%) per CBRE ([CBRE 2024 lessons](https://www.cbre.com/insights/articles/all-eyes-on-operating-costs-in-2025-lessons-learned-in-2024)). Per-room annual amenity spend specifically: scrape pending — STR Global / HotStats reports paywalled.

**Purchase triggers.** Guest experience differentiation. "Quality amenities enhance the guest experience and reflect positively on a brand" ([Hotel Tech Report procurement](https://hoteltechreport.com/news/hotel-procurement-company)). Sustainability + green certifications increasingly required for FF&E ([Beyer Brown FF&E](https://www.beyerbrown.com)). EWG Verified + design-forward bottle hits both signals (clean + Instagrammable for guest UGC).

**Brand affinities.** Beekman 1802, Aesop (in select luxury), Le Labo (Edition hotels), Public Goods, Grown Alchemist. Local makers ("locally made soaps or eco-friendly toiletries" cited as boutique differentiator) ([Lodgify amenities checklist](https://www.lodgify.com/blog/airbnb-essentials-guest-experience/)).

**Refill subscription willingness.** High structural fit. Procurement orgs prefer recurring contracts with managed restocking; STR property managers value auto-replenish to avoid stockouts between turnovers. Specific B2B refill subscription adoption %: scrape pending — no public dataset.

**Content sources.** Hotel Management Magazine, Hospitality Net, Skift, BoF Beauty. STR hosts: AirHosts Forum, BiggerPockets STR forum, Hostfully, Hospitable, AirDNA blog, IG @theairbnbcollection.

## Synthesis: GESINE Buyer Overlap (200 words)

The GESINE buyer is not three separate personas — it is one psychographic with three life-stage expressions. Across all three:

**Income floor.** All three sit at $100K+ HHI (DINK 61%, Anthropologie 28-45 mom $150-200K, hospitality buyer = procurement budget holder). This puts GESINE squarely in the top quartile of US households (top 23.9% per Census 2023). $78 SKU + refill is positioned correctly.

**Shared value stack.** EWG Verified (clean credibility) + scent-led (the editorial hook Aesop owns) + design-forward bottle (the C&B + Anthropologie + boutique hotel shelf-ready signal). All three personas pay premium for the trifecta — the wellness pro pays for safety + scent, the homemaker pays for shelf aesthetic + safety, the hospitality buyer pays for guest-perceivable luxury + sustainability cert.

**Channel logic.** DTC Q4 2026 captures personas 1 + 2 (urban pro + millennial mom shop direct after social discovery — 84-90% influenced by social per Sprout). Crate & Barrel 2027 anchors persona 2 trust + opens persona 3 hospitality discovery (boutique buyers source designer brands they see at C&B-tier retail).

**Refill economics.** All three over-index on subscription willingness (26% category growth + 12-18% sustainable premium tolerance per Mordor). The refill model is not a feature — it is the unifying buyer behavior across personas.
