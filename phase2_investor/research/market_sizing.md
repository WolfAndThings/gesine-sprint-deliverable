Sources: Statista, https://www.statista.com/statistics/1238231/aesop-revenue/ ; Glossy, https://www.glossy.co/beauty/aesop-ceo-michael-okeeffe-were-growing-at-double-the-global-rate-of-the-market/

Source: L'Oréal press release, https://www.loreal.com/en/press-release/group/l-oreal-completes-acquisition-of-aesop/ ; CNN Business, https://www.cnn.com/2023/04/04/business/australia-aesop-loreal-natura-deal-intl-hnk/index.html
