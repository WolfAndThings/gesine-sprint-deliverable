# Wholesale Line Sheet Template

What retail buyers (Goop, Anthropologie, Net-a-Porter, MoMA Design Store, et al) need when they evaluate a brand. Crate & Barrel deal already locked, [research/crate_barrel_intel.md](research/crate_barrel_intel.md). This template is for the next 6-8 retail conversations.

This document is the spec. Designer assembles the actual PDF in the design pass.

## Format

- 4-6 page PDF, printable letter or A4
- Brand-style cover page (1)
- Company snapshot (1)
- Product range with images, SKU codes, MSRP, wholesale (1-2)
- Order minimums, terms, shipping (1)
- Contact + signature block (1)

Total: 5 pages target. Adjustable.

## Page 1. Cover

- Brand mark (chosen palette)
- "GESINE Wholesale, Spring 2027" or relevant collection name

## Buyer pitch sequence (when sending line sheet)

1. Email intro: 100 words. One paragraph on the brand, one paragraph on the C&B locked partnership, one line ask.
2. Attach line sheet PDF.
3. Offer to send physical sample kit (Hero Bottle + 3 vials + ritual card).
4. If interest: schedule 30-min call.
5. After call: send terms sheet + initial PO mock.
