## Naming rules used

- 2-3 words max
- One pun OR one sensory anchor (never both)
- No body parts (Peel Good is borderline; OK because pop song)
- No "smart" puns that need explanation
- Drop any name that flinches when said in a Goop podcast
- Drop any name that sounds like a Tide variant

## What I avoided and why

- **Sponge Bath** - too on-nose, also kid product association
- **Pillow Talk** - Charlotte Tilbury owns it
- **Iron Lady** - political baggage
- **Lather, Rinse, Repeat** - too obvious, becomes filler
- **Foam Sweet Foam** - cute but feels Etsy-craft
