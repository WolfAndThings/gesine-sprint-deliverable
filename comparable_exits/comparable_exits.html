The takeaway

Two recent C&D deals set the GESINE bar.

Hero Cosmetics ($630M, 2022) and Touchland ($880M, 2025) are the cleanest comps. Both female-founded, design-led, scent-or-skin-adjacent, EBITDA-positive at exit, scaled DTC-then-retail. Both went to Church & Dwight. GESINE — luxury home reset bottle, EWG Verified, Crate & Barrel 2027 — is the same archetype, the same buyer, the same ~5x sales lane. Drunk Elephant's 8.5x at Shiseido is the upside slide; Aesop's $2.525B at L'Oréal is the long-horizon ceiling.
