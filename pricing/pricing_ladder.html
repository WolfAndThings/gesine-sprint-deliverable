Why $78 is the right price
GESINE is not
an $78 cleaning spray.
GESINE is an $78 ritual kit — sculptural bottle, scent concentrates, display holder, bamboo towels, refill system. At $78, GESINE doesn't compete with Clorox. It competes with the candle on the shelf, the hand soap on the sink, the cookware on the counter. The price only reads as expensive when the buyer thinks "cleaner." It reads as a deal when the buyer thinks "ritual kit."

Three pricing zones

$78 sits in the
premium ritual zone.

Cleaning has a commodity price ceiling around $25. Premium ritual products sit between $40 and $90. Luxury home objects start at $90 and go up. GESINE sits inside the premium ritual band — the same band as Aesop hand wash, Le Labo, Diptyque, and Flamingo Estate.

Zone 01 · Commodity
$3 – $25
Clorox, Lysol, Method, Mrs. Meyers, grocery refills. Volume-driven, shelf-driven, function-led copy. GESINE never enters this zone — it would collapse the brand.
Zone 02 · Premium ritual
$40 – $90
Aesop, DedCool, Flamingo Estate, Byredo, Diptyque, Le Labo. Object pricing, scent-led copy, gifting-grade packaging. GESINE owns the home-care slot in this band.
Zone 03 · Luxury system
$95 – $670+
Caraway sets, Our Place sets, Le Labo limited bottles, hospitality kits. Sets and systems justify the price. GESINE expansion path — gifting kits and collab drops live here.
