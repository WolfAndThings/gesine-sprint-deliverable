08 · the buyer · one psychographic, three lives

$100K+ HHI floor across all three.

"Who's the customer?" is investor question #2 (after market size). The honest answer for GESINE is that there's one Modern Premium Consumer who shows up in three life-stage expressions. The wellness-led professional buys it for herself. The aesthetic-forward homemaker buys it because Crate & Barrel curated it. The hospitality buyer specs it for 100 hotel rooms because guests will Instagram it. All three over-index on subscription willingness, all three sit above $100K HHI (top 23.9% of US households per Census 2023), and all three respond to the same EWG-Verified-plus-scent-plus-design value stack. The three personas aren't independent markets — they're the same buyer at different ages and contexts.

One brand build, three GTM motions.
