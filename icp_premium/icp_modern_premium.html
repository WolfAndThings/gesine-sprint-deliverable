The GESINE buyer is not three separate personas — it is one Modern Premium Consumer with three life-stage expressions. Income floor: $100K+ HHI across all three (top 23.9% of US households per Census 2023 P60-282). Shared value stack: EWG Verified + scent-led + design-forward bottle. All three over-index on refill subscription willingness — the unifying buyer behavior.

Persona 01

Wellness-led Professional.

SINGLE / DINK · URBAN
HHI Band
$100K-$250K. Goop core averages $100K+ HHI. 61% of DINK households earn $100K+. 23.9% of all US households cleared $150K in 2023 (Census P60-282).
Age Band
25-44. Glossier's largest visitor cluster 25-34. Aesop core 25-44 (skewing 20-55 educated urban). Goop original target 25-45.
Household
Single urban professional or DINK. DINKs = 5% of US households per Harris Poll Nov 2024 — spend 4x more on dining ($816 vs $215/mo) and ~$2,000/vacation.
Where They Shop
Aesop (395 stores in 29 markets at acquisition; sales went $28M to $537M 2012-2022). Goop.com, Sephora premium, Credo, Violet Grey. Direct DTC for Glossier.
Wellness Spend
McKinsey: US wellness market $480B, growing 5-10% YoY. Gen Z + millennial = 36% of population, 41% of wellness spend. 82% of US consumers rank wellness top/important priority.
Brand Affinities
Aesop · Le Labo · Diptyque · Goop · Glossier · Necessaire · Public Goods. Goop newsletter readers avg age 34, $100K+ HHI.
Content
IG + TikTok dominant. 90% of Gen Z and 84% of millennials say social content influenced a purchase in last 6 months (Sprout Social 2025). 41% of Gen Z search social before Google. Pubs: The Cut, Vogue, The Strategist, Cup of Jo.
Triggers
EWG Verified mark = "gold standard of clean safety" (Clean Beauty Cos.). 46% of US consumers (53% Gen Z) spent more on cosmetic procedures in 2024 than 2023 (McKinsey).
Refill Sub Willingness
Auto-refill grew 26% YoY. 47% of consumers choose refillable packaging. Willing to pay 12-18% premium for sustainable options (Mordor).
Persona 02

Aesthetic-forward Homemaker.

MILLENNIAL MOM · DESIGN-DRIVEN
HHI Band
$150K-$200K. Anthropologie cites 28-45 college-educated professional women, married, kids, $150-200K HHI.
Age Band
28-45. Anthropologie.com largest visitor cluster 25-34. Crate & Barrel skews older — 35-44 + 45-54 dominance (Statista 2018).
Household
Married, 1-3 kids, suburban or urban-suburban. Professional or ex-professional.
Where They Shop
Crate & Barrel (Furniture/Homeware = 95% of 2025 sales). Anthropologie home, West Elm, Pottery Barn, McGee & Co, Jenni Kayne Home, Boll & Branch, Schoolhouse, Brooklinen. Whole Foods + Erewhon. Williams-Sonoma + Food52.
Home Spend
Millennials spend ~$1,771/yr on home decor (~$148/mo), 23% above Boomers (SwiftBeacon). Millennials averaged $12,101 on home projects in 2024 (Angi State of Home Spending).
Brand Affinities
Brooklinen · Boll & Branch · Parachute · Le Labo Santal 33 candles · Diptyque · Maison Louis Marie. Non-toxic clean: Branch Basics · Blueland · Grove.
Content
Pinterest dominant for home research. 47% of millennials buy furniture online after weeks of research; 75% check prices at multiple retailers; >50% of millennial moms use social to inform purchases (iposos 2024). Pubs: Domino, AD, Apartment Therapy, Magnolia Journal. IG: @studiomcgee · @amberinteriors · @athenacalderone. TikTok: #cleantok · #homereset · #sundayreset.
Triggers
Sustainability + ethical production = significant decision factors (Capital One Shopping). EWG Verified resonates strongly given child-safety framing. Crate & Barrel 2027 placement = the credibility marker — millennial moms research multi-retailer and trust C&B taste curation.
Refill Sub Willingness
Strong. Over-indexes given household consumption volume + cabinet-aesthetic motivation. Branch Basics + Blueland confirm subscription economics.
Persona 03

Hospitality / Amenity Buyer.

BOUTIQUE HOTEL OPS · STR HOST
Segment Size
Boutique hotel market $26.68B in 2024 → $40.26B by 2030, 7.2% CAGR (Grand View Research). US Airbnb hosts earned $24B+ supplemental in 2024; typical host earned ~$14,000; entire-unit avg $305/night.
Buyer Profile
Two sub-segments: (a) boutique hotel GM / procurement lead sourcing FF&E and amenities; (b) Airbnb / STR host operating 1-10 properties. Boutique buyers source via specialty procurement (R-W Purchasing, Beyer Brown, SLX Hospitality, Astria).
Where They Buy
Premium brand-name suppliers (APOTECAROMA, EAU D'EDEN, Beekman 1802, White Label) for hotel toiletries. STR hosts buy on Amazon, Costco, direct DTC. Minoan platform connects STR hosts to home brands for in-unit shoppable experiences.
Per-Property Spend
Avg consumables ~$4.27/night per Airbnb host calc (2019, low confidence). Boutique 2024 cost increases: agency commissions (6.0%) and complimentary F&B (3.9%) per CBRE. Per-room annual amenity spend specifically: scrape pending — STR Global / HotStats paywalled.
Triggers
Guest experience differentiation. "Quality amenities enhance the guest experience and reflect positively on a brand" (Hotel Tech Report). Sustainability + green certifications increasingly required for FF&E. EWG Verified + design-forward bottle hits both signals (clean + Instagrammable for guest UGC).
Brand Affinities
Beekman 1802 · Aesop (in select luxury) · Le Labo (Edition hotels) · Public Goods · Grown Alchemist. Local makers ("locally made soaps or eco-friendly toiletries" = boutique differentiator).
Content
Hotel Management Magazine · Hospitality Net · Skift · BoF Beauty. STR: AirHosts Forum · BiggerPockets STR · Hostfully · Hospitable · AirDNA blog · IG @theairbnbcollection.
Refill Sub Willingness
High structural fit. Procurement orgs prefer recurring contracts with managed restocking; STR property managers value auto-replenish to avoid stockouts between turnovers. Specific B2B refill subscription adoption %: scrape pending — no public dataset.
Synthesis · the unifying buyer

$100K+ HHI · EWG + scent + design · refill loyal.

The GESINE buyer is one Modern Premium Consumer, three expressions. Income floor: $100K+ HHI across all three (top 23.9% of US households). $78 set + refill positioned correctly. Shared value stack: EWG Verified (clean credibility) + scent-led (Aesop's editorial hook) + design-forward bottle (the C&B / Anthropologie / boutique-hotel shelf-ready signal). All three pay premium for the trifecta. Channel logic: DTC Aug/Sept 2026 captures personas 1+2 (urban pro + millennial mom shop direct after social discovery — 84-90% influenced by social per Sprout). Crate & Barrel 2027 anchors persona 2 trust + opens persona 3 hospitality discovery (boutique buyers source designer brands they see at C&B-tier retail). Refill economics: All three over-index on subscription willingness (26% category growth + 12-18% sustainable premium tolerance). Refill is not a feature — it is the unifying buyer behavior.
