CONCEPT 01
Bedside Table Reset
Hero shot · D2C web
Setting
Walnut nightstand, charcoal linen sheet edge in frame, single brass-pull lamp with warm 2700K glow. Brooklyn loft window light bouncing off the wall.
Mood
End-of-day exhale. Quiet, slow, expensive but lived-in. The exact second before the lamp clicks off.
Surface palette
Walnut grain · charcoal linen · cream wall paint · brass detail.
Camera notes
85mm equivalent, eye-level with the bottle, shallow f/2.0 with falloff to the lamp. Real grain, slight haze from a 2700K source.
Use case
Hero shot for D2C nightstand SKU page · paid social static · Sunday Reset newsletter.
◆ Prompt-ready brief NanoBanana 2 · 2:3
Editorial still life. Sculptural matte ceramic spray bottle in bone color sitting on a walnut nightstand. Beside it: a single brass-pull lamp casting warm 2700K light, a folded charcoal linen sheet edge in frame. Cream wall in soft falloff behind. Brooklyn loft, late evening, window dark. Shot on 85mm equivalent at f/2.0, slight grain, real Kodak Portra texture, no AI-perfect surfaces. The bottle is the hero, the lamp is the cue. End-of-day exhale. No logos. Vertical composition.
