Every prompt below is paste-ready for ChatGPT (Image Gen 4), with NanoBanana 2 fallback when ChatGPT moderation trips. Locked brand world

Same world.
Fifteen rooms in it.

Every prompt inherits these rules. Don't let any single prompt drift — if a result looks off, the world reference is what to tighten before re-prompting.

GESINE world specification
BottleSculptural sprayer in smoked-amber or frosted glass. Soft ergonomic curve. Matte soft-touch cap in deep olive or warm cream. ~6 inches tall. Looks designed, not branded — the silhouette carries the identity.
Materials in frameTravertine, honed marble, oak, walnut, brushed brass, raw linen, bamboo. Soft cotton, ceramic, weathered stone. Avoid plastic, neon, glossy white, anything cold or clinical.
LightSoft natural daylight from a window, late-afternoon golden, or warm interior lamp glow. Always one directional source, never overhead beauty light. Subtle film grain. Slight warm cast.
PaletteWarm cream, soft amber, deep olive, sage, smoked black. Accents of grapefruit, tomato leaf green, lavender muted. Never pastel, never high-saturation.
CompositionEditorial, asymmetric. Hands or partial human in frame on most shots. Off-center bottle. Negative space generous. Shot like a Kinfolk spread, not a product catalog.
Banned"Beautiful," "stunning," "perfect," "8K," "hyperrealistic," "luxury," "premium" — never in prompt text. Pristine product render energy. Floating-on-white commercial framing. AI tells (extra fingers, plastic skin).

How to run all 15 in parallel
1. Generate 06 (Sculptural Hero — Bottle Silhouette) first, alone, 5–8 times. Pick the strongest silhouette result. Save it as the master bottle reference.

2. For prompts 01–05 and 07–15, append a final line: "The bottle in this scene matches the silhouette in [reference image attached]." Attach the master image. This locks bottle consistency across all 15 outputs.

3. Run the remaining 14 in parallel batches of 5 in ChatGPT. Total time: ~25 minutes including re-rolls.

4. Pick the strongest 1 from each prompt → 15 final web-ready images. Pick the top 2–3 → animate via Veo 3.1 with the per-prompt animation note for short-form video tests.

5. If ChatGPT moderation refuses any prompt, fallback engine is NanoBanana 2 via kie.ai. Re-prompt by softening any time-of-day specifics ("9pm" → "evening") and removing brand-comparison cues ("similar to Aesop" → just "next to a tall apothecary bottle").
