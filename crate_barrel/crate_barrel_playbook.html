Imprint Visa loyalty · launched Oct 2025

The refill subscription accelerator.

C&B + CB2 + Crate & Kids + Hudson Grace cardholders get 10% back in Reward Dollars on every banner purchase. Cardholders are repeat, high-LTV C&B shoppers. If GESINE Organizer Set links to refills (capsules $18 · cloths $18), cardholders earn 10% back on every refill order. This drives subscription-style behavior C&B currently lacks in this category — and sets up GESINE as the proof case for refill economics inside the Imprint Visa program. Pitch: card-linked auto-refill is the unlock.

Pricing tiers: room sprays $24.95-$49.95, scented candles $36-$84, taper sets $34.95-$109.95. $78 GESINE Organizer Set slots cleanly. Smoked Sage colorway differentiates against the cream/amber-dominant wall. No premium ritual brand owns the cleaning-meets-fragrance crossover at C&B today — GESINE's whitespace.
